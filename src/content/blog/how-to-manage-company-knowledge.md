Every manufacturing company faces the same challenge: the knowledge that drives daily work exists mainly in employees' heads.

## The problem with knowledge in people's heads

When an experienced employee goes on vacation or leaves the company, they take years of experience with them. Procedures they knew by heart suddenly become inaccessible to everyone else.

## The solution: an electronic knowledge base

OneDoc turns knowledge from employees' heads into an accessible, searchable company resource. Every article can be edited with one click, and AI helps with content formulation.

## Key benefits

- New employees onboard faster
- Knowledge doesn't leave with the employee
- Fewer questions to colleagues = more time for actual work
