## Challenge

KLL Informatyka delivers heavily customised ERP systems and dedicated applications. Each deployment had a different configuration, and knowledge about each client's specifics lived exclusively in the heads of consultants.

## Solution

We deployed OneDoc as an internal knowledge base for the team. Each project received its own branch with configuration documentation, support procedures and change history.

OneDoc's unique permission-group system allows us to maintain a single master product documentation and create client-specific branches by toggling individual articles on or off. As a result, each client sees up-to-date documentation tailored precisely to their KLL-ERP installation.

## Results

- A new consultant can handle a client independently without asking the team
- Employee onboarding time reduced several times over
- Ability to switch to the client's perspective at any time
