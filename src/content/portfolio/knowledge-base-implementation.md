## Challenge

A company with 80 production floor employees struggled with knowledge transfer. Every new employee had to shadow experienced colleagues for several weeks.

## Solution

We implemented OneDoc as a central knowledge base. We imported existing Word documents, structured them, and added an AI agent.

## Results

- **40%** shorter onboarding time for new employees
- **60%** fewer questions directed to supervisors
- Full documentation of 120 production procedures
