## Challenge

Nordflam manufactures fireplaces and heating devices that require strict quality control at multiple production stages. QC procedures were stored in Word documents and PDFs, making them difficult to update and access on the shop floor.

## Solution

We deployed OneDoc as the central system for quality control procedures. Each production stage has its own checklist, and any changes are immediately visible to everyone.

## Results

- Standardised QC procedures — easily accessible and always up to date
- Reduction in finished product defects
- Shorter internal audit times
