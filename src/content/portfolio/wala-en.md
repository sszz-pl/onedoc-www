## Challenge

Wala had dozens of workstation instructions and PDF files scattered across network folders. Onboarding new employees at this fast-growing company required a systematic approach to knowledge transfer.

## Solution

Employees consolidated and migrated the instructions into OneDoc, creating a clear structure organised by workstation and machine. Every instruction is now findable through the search in seconds.

## Results

- Employee knowledge transferred into OneDoc
- Employees find the procedures they need independently
- Instruction updates visible instantly to the entire team
